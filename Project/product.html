iPhone 12 Pro 64GB

The iPhone 12 Pro 64GB is available in four stunning colors: Silver, Graphite, Gold, and Pacific Blue.

It features a 6.1-inch Super Retina XDR display, powered by Apple's A14 Bionic chip, offering outstanding performance.

With its 64GB of internal storage, you can store a wealth of photos, videos, and apps.

Capture professional-quality photos and videos with its advanced triple-camera system, including ultra-wide, wide, and telephoto lenses, all at 12-megapixels.

Experience lightning-fast 5G connectivity and secure Face ID facial recognition.

The iPhone 12 Pro 64GB runs on the latest iOS, ensuring you have access to the latest features and updates.
